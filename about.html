Built By Practitioners, Not Just Programmers

InfraBid was developed in conjunction with construction professionals, AI researchers, project management and estimating experts, and PhD researchers working across academia and industry — so the platform reflects how tenders are actually won, not how a spreadsheet thinks they should be.

Four disciplines, one estimating engine

Every module in InfraBid — the valuation model, the takeoff tooling, the merchant pricing feeds — was shaped by people who have to live with the consequences of an estimate being wrong.

Construction Professionals

Site managers, quantity surveyors, and contractors who reviewed every rate, ledger line, and dossier format against real tenders they've actually submitted.

AI Researchers

Applied machine-learning researchers responsible for the parts of the platform that read documents and detect structure — and for being honest about what that technology can and can't do yet.

Project Management & Estimating Experts

Practitioners who defined how a valuation model should behave under real margin pressure, procurement deadlines, and client scrutiny.

PhD Researchers

Academic researchers working across construction economics, quantity surveying, and applied AI, contributing methodology review and validation from outside the commercial team.
