How this works: InfraBid renders your actual PDF or image file to canvas in your browser — nothing is uploaded to a server. You calibrate the scale by drawing a line over any element of known real-world length (a dimension line, a door width, a grid spacing) and entering that length. Every length, area, and count you then trace is an exact geometric calculation against that calibrated scale — not an automated estimate. Accuracy depends entirely on how precisely you calibrate and trace, the same as measuring on a printed drawing with a scale rule.
