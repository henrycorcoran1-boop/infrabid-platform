Tender Import

Upload the client's pricing document, specifications, and drawings. Since the quantities are already measured in the pricing document, InfraBid extracts the bill of quantities directly and prices it against your rate library — no re-measuring required.

01 / Upload Documents

Pricing Document

The client's Bill of Quantities / Schedule of Rates — CSV or Excel parses most reliably; PDF is supported but best-effort.

Specifications

Attached for reference alongside this tender — not parsed for quantities in this version.

Drawings

Attached for reference. Need to measure something the BOQ doesn't cover? Use AI Takeoff separately.

How this works: Everything is parsed and priced entirely in your browser — no file is uploaded to a server. The pricing document's line items (description, unit, quantity) are extracted automatically and matched against your rate library by description. CSV/Excel extraction is reliable; PDF extraction is heuristic (text-position based) and should always be reviewed line by line before you rely on it.
